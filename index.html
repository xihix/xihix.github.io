Introduction

Welcome to my homepage! I am currently a graduate student in the School of Information Science and Technology at ShanghaiTech University, with my advisor Prof. Ziyu Shao. My current research focuses on the design and analysis of optimization algorithms for interesting problems in data center networks. Before being at ShanghaiTech, I received my B.S. degree in software engineering at Nanjing University. I do enjoy the research and study life here at ShanghaiTech!

Research Interests

At present, my research interest concentrates on the design and analysis of distributed scheduling algorithms in data center networks, so as to make the trade-off between the cost of network failures and bandwidth reduction, as well as other interesting problems in data center networks. Welcome any discussion over related topics, and you can get contact with me via any of the following ways!
